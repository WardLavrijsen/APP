About PMU
PMU is a power sensor made for all house or business owners. The sensor measures the power usage of the device that is plugged into the sensor. With the data gathered from measuring the usage we calculate a few points of data to keep you updated on your data. These points are, the usage in Apms, the usage in Watts, the power Watt-hours and the total price of power used by the plugged in device. Lastly we also calculate the CO2 usage of the device.
