Step 1: Download the PMU app
Download the PMU app, for Apple devices available in the App store. For Android devices available in the Google Play store.
Step 2: Run the app and create an account

Press the Don’t have an account? Register Now button on the bottom of the Login page.

Now you are getting redirected to the Register page.

Fill in your E-mailadress, password, your password again and optional your Energy price in € per kwH.

When done press the Sign up button.

Step 3: Add your first device

Press the

icon on the home page in the

app. Your camera will open now.

With the camera opened scan the QR-code on your PMU.

The app will automatically read the QR-code and an Add Device menu will show up. In this menu you can place the PMU in a category and give it a name.

When done with giving the PMU a category and a name press the green Add Device button.

Step 4: Reading your power usage

Your added devices will automatically be shown on te home page.

On the right side of a device block you can see the current power usage in Watts.

If you want more information about your power usage, Co2 emissions and costs tap on the device block.

The device block will open and show you more information.
